What does Kanaka Solar Power Grid Do?

This website aims to promote and advertise the use of a clean and portable energy source for schools and entrepreneurs in the remote areas that unfortunately do not have a major power plant nearby to supply electricity.

The Kanaka Solar Power Grid is a technology that traps sunlight during the day in small micro grids. The energy is then stored on a ‘smart’ battery box which then powers small houses. This in turn will ensure children can study in the night using light and small retailers can keep fish and other organic food supply ‘fresh’ and cool.

This website will highlight the benefits of said tech and how it can sustain a healthy and efficient lifestyle for the rural areas of PNG .
